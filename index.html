About Manning's Equation

Manning's equation is used to calculate the flow in open channels:

V = (1.49/n) × R2/3 × S1/2

Where:

  • V = Velocity (ft/s)
  • n = Manning's roughness coefficient
  • R = Hydraulic radius (ft) = Area / Wetted perimeter
  • S = Channel slope (ft/ft)

The discharge Q (cfs) is calculated using:

Q = A × V

Where A is the flow area (m²).
